This specification outlines the "Detailed Dog Adoption Profiles" feature, a cornerstone of the Bali Dog Rescue platform. Each profile serves as a digital biography for a rescue dog, designed to create an emotional connection with potential adopters and provide all necessary information for informed decisions. The feature will support rich media, including multiple high-resolution photos and videos, showcasing the dog's personality and appearance. A comprehensive biography section will tell the dog's unique story, detailing its background, rescue journey, and any special needs. Key data points like current health status (e.g., vaccinations, spay/neuter status, medical conditions), age, gender, size, and temperament notes will be clearly displayed. Admins will have robust tools to easily create, edit, and update these profiles, including managing media uploads and adoption status. For potential adopters, a clear "Apply to Adopt" call-to-action will be prominently featured, directly linking to the adoption application process. This feature is critical for increasing adoption rates by providing transparency and fostering deep engagement, ultimately connecting more Bali street dogs with loving forever homes globally.
