The Home Page (`/`) serves as the primary entry point and digital storefront for Bali Dog Rescue. Its purpose is to immediately convey the organization's mission, showcase its impact, and guide users towards key actions: adopting, donating, or volunteering. The page will feature a prominent hero section with a compelling mission statement and captivating imagery, immediately drawing visitors in. Below this, a dynamic "Featured Adoptable Dogs" section will highlight a rotating selection of dogs urgently seeking homes, with direct links to their individual profiles. An "Impact & Success Stories" snippet section will demonstrate the tangible results of the organization's work, building trust and inspiring action. Quick links and clear calls-to-action for 'Adopt', 'Donate', and 'Volunteer' will be strategically placed throughout the page, ensuring easy navigation to core functionalities. A concise "About Us/Our Story" summary will provide context and foster an emotional connection with the organization's values. The design will be modern, clean, and welcoming, reflecting the compassionate and hopeful vibe of the project, with a focus on user engagement and clear pathways to support Bali's street dogs.
