This flow outlines the comprehensive journey a potential adopter undertakes to apply for a dog through the Bali Dog Rescue platform. It begins when a logged-in user, having explored the available dogs, decides to take the next step towards adoption. The user first navigates to the 'Adoptable Dogs' page, utilizing filters and search functionalities to browse various profiles. Upon finding a dog of interest, they proceed to view its detailed profile page, absorbing the rich media and comprehensive information provided. A prominent 'Apply to Adopt' button on the dog's profile initiates the application process. The user is then guided through a multi-step application form, where they provide essential personal details, information about their living situation, and their experience with dogs. After reviewing all entered information, the user submits the completed application. The system then provides an immediate confirmation of receipt, sends a confirmation email to the user, and updates the application's status within the user's personalized dashboard. Concurrently, the system notifies the Bali Dog Rescue administrators of the new application, prompting their review process. This flow ensures a structured, transparent, and user-friendly application experience, vital for successful adoptions.
